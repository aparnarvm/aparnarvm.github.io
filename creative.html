About Me

With over a decade dedicated to mastering the tabla under the guidance of maestros like Prakash Vengeri Sir, Ustad Rafiyuddin Sabri and Anubratha Chatterjee, I've immersed myself in a symphony of rhythms. My journey in Carnatic music commenced at the age of three, and beyond the tabla, I play drums, violin, thakil, dhol, and love experimenting with konnakol.

My pursuit extends beyond mastery; I seek to compose and experiment with Hindustani, Carnatic and Fusion music.
